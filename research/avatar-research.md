## SKEPTICISM TRIGGERS

What makes them doubt a solution will work:

- "Too good to be true"
- "Sounds like another gimmick"
- "I've tried so many things"
- "How is this different?"
- "Is this a scam?"
- "Are these reviews real?"
- "Another influencer product"

### How They Overcome Skepticism
- Real reviews with specific details
- Understanding the mechanism (WHY it works)
- Money-back guarantee
- Seeing before/after timelines
- Authentic-sounding testimonials (typos, raw emotion)
